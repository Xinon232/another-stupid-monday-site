Verified product facts

  • Name: Another Stupid Monday
  • Creator: Halim Jarrar, psychotherapist
  • Format: One small task each week, beginning Monday
  • Complete journey: 100 tasks
  • Approach: No daily streaks, no punishment, and no pressure to catch up
  • Availability: iPhone and iPad through the Apple App Store
  • Current verified App Store version: 1.4.0
  • Business model: First Monday free; future Mondays available through a one-time Lifetime purchase

Short description

Another Stupid Monday is a gentle wellbeing and self-reflection companion that offers one small task each week. Tasks remain available when unfinished, so there is no streak to protect and no pressure to catch up.
