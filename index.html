How it works

A slower kind of progress.

There are 100 tasks in the journey, but you never need to think about all of them. The point is not to finish quickly. The point is to do what is in front of you this week.

01

Monday arrives

A new small task is waiting for you at the start of the week.

02

Take your time

Check in during the week. Do it in your own way and at your own pace.

03

Move on when ready

Complete the task to continue. If not, it stays with you—without punishment.

Who it is for

Healthier habits, without the hard sell.

Another Stupid Monday is for people who want a small, weekly prompt for self-care, reflection, connection, or one manageable action—without streaks, guilt, daily targets, or pressure to catch up.

You do not need to become a new person by Friday. You only need to meet the week you are actually in.

The idea
“Monday is coming anyway.
You only need one small thing.”

Small helpful actions can be easier to begin—and to return to—than trying to change everything at once.

Made with care

Created by a real psychotherapist.

The tasks, their order, and the small dialogues around them are crafted by Halim Jarrar, psychotherapist.

Another Stupid Monday is a companion for self-reflection and everyday wellbeing. It is not a substitute for psychotherapy, diagnosis, treatment, or crisis support.

There is no falling behind.If a task is unfinished, it simply remains there. Start where you are.
